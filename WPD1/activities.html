Our Water Sports Activities

We offer tuition in canoeing, kayaking, sailing, water-skiing and rafting together with all the latest equipment to suit every ability .If you are already accomplished in your chosen sport, you are welcome to hire equipment independently and just take to the water but if you would like to improve your skills, we offer a range of RYA courses and one-to-one private tuition.

White Water Rafting

On the river you can experience grade 3 and 4 rapids with 6 or 8 in a raft with qualified and professional guides. Wakeboarding Try wakeboarding with a fantastic mountain back drop. Water skiing and windsurfing are also available.Kayaking For all the family take a kayak Sailing With first class tuition from Royal Yachting Association (RYA) instructors and a superb selection of sailing craft you can learn to sail while enjoying amazing scenery . We have a range of equipment for children right up to seasoned professionals. These include; Oppies, Bugs, Teras, Picos, Topazes, Lasers, Bahias, Vagos, Dart 16s, CX16s, RS 500s and Laser 400.

Scuba Diving

Discover the impressively colourful underwater world on our diving holidays for the whole family. The beautifully calm waters of our resort is perfect for exploring the underwater world and our PADI registered dive school has a number of courses available for you to experience scuba diving whether you are just starting out as a complete beginner or already hold a diving qualification.

Water-skiing

Informal instruction from our dedicated in-house ski team, using all the latest equipment. We have standard and wide combo skis for beginners and a good selection of mono skis, wakeboards, children's skis and kneeboards. Plus we provide training bars for beginners and in some resorts extended ski poles for wakeboarders.
